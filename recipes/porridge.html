Porridge Recipe

This not so common meal is believed to have gotten his root from the Ijebu part of the yoruba people in Nigeria.

Some might even argue that it is more common among the Ekiti people of Nigeria, this shows how vast and wide this meal is enjoyed in Nigeria

The meal is made with over boiled yam with different ingredients added depending on choice.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Maggie
  • Salt
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Palm oil
  • onions
  • Vegetable

Steps

  1. Cut Yam
  2. Boil yam for about 25 minutes
  3. Add diced onions
  4. Add other ingredients of choice
  5. Add palm oil
  6. Add chopped vegetables
  7. Allow to cook for few minutes
  8. Dish is ready to be served
