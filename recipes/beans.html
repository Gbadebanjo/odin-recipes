Beans Recipe

A life saver meal is what beans can be best described as in Nigeria.

The beauty of this amazing dish is that it can be taken along side a long list of variety of foods

There are quite series of controversial mixtures you can try out as well

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Maggie
  • Salt
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Palm oil
  • onions

Steps

  1. Wash beans with water
  2. Pour the washed beans in a pot and allow to boil for 25 minutes
  3. Add diced onions
  4. Add other ingredients of choice
  5. Add palm oil
  6. Allow to cook for few minutes
  7. Dish is ready to be served
