Nigeria Jollof Rice Recipe

Nigeria jollof rice is a popular meal in Nigeria which in most cases is usually considered as a ceremonial dish of the nation

There have always been controversies between the Nigera Jollof and the Ghana jollof

Which do you think takes the lead?

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Maggie
  • Salt
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Vegetable oil
  • onions

Steps

  1. Pour the rice in a bowl
  2. Wash rice throughly with water
  3. Add oil to pot and allow to steam for about two minutes
  4. Add diced onions to pot
  5. Add pepper mix
  6. Add Maggie
  7. Add Thyme
  8. Add Curry
  9. Add salt
  10. Allow mix to boil for about 5 minutes
  11. Add washed rice to pot
  12. Allow to steam and boil for about 20 minutes
  13. Stir and taste
  14. Serve and enjoy meal with any protein of your choice
